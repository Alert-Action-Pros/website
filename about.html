From Frustration to a Full-Fledged Solution

AlertAction Pros was founded in 2025 by a team that knows the struggle — because we lived it.

As merchants ourselves, we constantly battled the exhausting routine of managing chargeback alerts. Every missed alert meant lost revenue. Every delayed action meant a higher risk of disputes. It wasn't just stressful — it was unsustainable.

We searched for a reliable solution that could take the weight off our shoulders. But what we found was a gap in the market: services that were slow, impersonal, or too complex to integrate. So we decided to build what we wished we had.

Why We Exist

We created AlertAction Pros to give merchants one simple promise:

"We handle your chargeback alerts — so you don't have to."

Our goal? Let you focus on running and growing your business — while we handle the alerts, actions, and reporting behind the scenes.

Whether you're a startup or a high-volume merchant, our team of experts ensures alerts are acted on in real time — saving you time, money, and disputes.
